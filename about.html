About Us

Dependable Aviation Consulting (DAC) is a division of Dependable Outcomes Ltd (DOL), a privately owned Ugandan company based in Kampala, Uganda.

At DAC we strive to offer our clients result-oriented solutions in airline management consulting, chartering, leasing and outsourcing of aircraft spare parts and maintenance contracts. Our team of experts has the industry competence in these fields and can provide tailored solutions to meet each client’s needs.

Given the rapid change of the African aviation landscape, our experts keep abreast of the changes and continue to pioneer innovative solutions to deliver successful projects and help our clients improve service. We collaborate with our clients to evaluate their needs and establish strategies for success.

Mission

Our philosophy is to serve each client with dedications in order to get results beyond their expectation by improving service, optimizing revenues or lowering costs.

Our Expertise

  • Network and Fleet Planning

  • Market Analysis and Forecasting

  • Chartering, Leasing and Purchasing

  • Outsourcing MRO Services and Spare parts.
